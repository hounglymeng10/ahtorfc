About Us
OUR
STORY

🏫 How It Started

AHTOR FC started as a group of high school students in Angkor Chum District who shared one passion — football. We played on dusty pitches, trained without proper kits, but never without spirit.

🎓 Where We Are Now

After graduating university, our founding members came back to build something real. Today AHTOR FC is a registered community club with 36 players, a coach, and a vision to grow into Cambodia's most respected grassroots club.

🚀 Where We Are Going

We are building a Youth Academy, competing in provincial leagues, and creating opportunities for young players in Siem Reap. By 2030, AHTOR FC will be a professional club — and we are just getting started.
